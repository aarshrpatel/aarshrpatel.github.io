Problem Statement:

Students and young adults have a hard time finding mentors and getting advice from people in the industry they want to go in. Getting connections and networking is hard for many students. Which makes it hard for them to find internships and research opportunities.

Affinity diagram: MentorHub

Our group worked together to search for ideas and solutions that would be related to our mentoring app and group these ideas.

Team Personas: MentorHub

My Persona was a recent graduat with a degree in Business Administration that is 22 years old. She is trying to get her first job and needs a way to connect with people that can help her.

Team Storyboards: MentorHub

My storyboard is on a recently graduated women who goes by the name Emily Parker. She is currently looking for a job and needs a mentor to guide her through the process.

Team Sketches: MentorHub
